About

MC's emergence into a solo artist is as daring as it is spontaneous. It’s the project of a 22-year-old singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and producer. She blends genres like alternative rock and pop, with lyrics that contain blunt honesty and dark humor. She spent years as a band member in the rock band Chopped Up Tulips, among other bands, before deciding it was time do start her own project. MC’s records take the modern elements of pop songwriting and recording, and mixes them with her rock influences to create a guitar-heavy catchy modern pop sound. Within weeks of the start of this project, her raw talent and uniquely catchy songs landed her a spot as the opening act for Drake Bell on his New Orleans tour stop. Since then, the fire has not stopped burning beneath her as she works every day to speak to the world through her music, with her live band, MC & The Manics.
